Spring Show

Notes for before you arrive — and when you get there.

Arrival

Doors open at 10:00am. It can help to arrive a little earlier if you would like time to get your bearings.

Parking

Parking is in the Yellow Car Park. From there, follow signs for the shuttle bus to the main entrance.

Finding your seat

Your place is in Hall 3, Block B, Row 12. Staff nearby should be able to help if you need a quiet pointer in the right direction.

Before you leave

When the show finishes, follow signs back to the shuttle buses for the Yellow Car Park. The route can feel a little disorientating, so it may help to pause and check the signs as you go.
